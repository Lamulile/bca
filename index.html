About Our Founder

Leadership with audit depth and commercial acumen.

Siphokazi Bantom, CA(SA)

Registered Chartered Accountant under the South African Institute of Chartered Accountants (SAICA) and a SARS‑registered tax practitioner. Holds a BCom in Accounting and a Certificate in the Theory of Accounting (BCom Hons/CTA).

Completed SAICA articles at a reputable Johannesburg audit firm, gaining practical experience in financial audits & independent reviews, preparation of financial statements, assisting clients with Revenue Services audits, and training junior clerks. Post‑articles, she gained commercial experience as an accountant at various multinationals, managing finance functions, ensuring compliance with financial controls, and providing decision‑ready management information.

She serves as an Independent Trustee and Chairperson of Audit & Risk Committee at Cookhouse Wind Farm Community Trust (Eastern Cape), and as an Independent Trustee at Khobab Renewable Energy Community Trust and Loeriesfontein Renewable Energy Community Trust (Northern Cape).
